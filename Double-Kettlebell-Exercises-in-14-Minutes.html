Double Kettlebell Exercises in 14 Minutes

It's obvious kettlebell exercises are excellent for losing fat, but the double kettlebell complex for weight loss is tough to beat, particularly when you' wish to strip fat off rapidly.

This video shows you how to establish this type of kettlebell fat loss workout, including some of the best kettlebell workouts to use.

Below is a description of the double kettlebell fat loss complex exercise demonstrated in the video:

Double Snatch x 30 seconds

Double Press x 30 seconds

Double Front Squat x 30s seconds

Double Swing x 30 seconds

+ Do this 3 times total. Rest 4 minutes between sets. Overall workout time: 14 minutes.

+ Utilize a light-to-medium pair of kettlebells (I'm using a pair of 24kgs).

+ Perform in a location you can drop the bells in case you lose your grip (rubber flooring or outside is best).

Here's a simple method to set up your own double kettlebell complex for fat loss:.

1. Select 3 to 6 double kettlebell workouts.

2. Perform 3 to 6 representatives per exercise.

3. Make the exercises "flow" together naturally like they carry out in this video.

4. Base the double kettlebell complex off your weakest workout.

5. Rest 2x-5x as long as the double kettlebell complex takes you to perform.

( NOTE: Advanced double kettlebell exercises in this video worth practicing on their own if you're lacking experience - Double kettlebell swing, double kettlebell snatch, double kettlebell front squat, double kettlebell press.)
