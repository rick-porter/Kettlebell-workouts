How To Perform The Double Kettlebell Front Squat

Here's how to double kettlebell front squat securely and effectively to get stronger, more powerful legs, and a tight midsection for overall body strength & conditioning.

1. Establish a set of kettlebells on the ground 6 to 12 inches in front of you like you would for the Double Kettlebell Swing.

2. Clean the kettlebells into the rack and get comfortable.

3. Reset your feet if required to just outside shoulder width. Feet may be pointed straight ahead or slightly turned out in between 15 and 30 degrees.

4. Keeping your abs tight, hold your breath and pull yourself down into the hole as far as you can without rounding your lower back.
