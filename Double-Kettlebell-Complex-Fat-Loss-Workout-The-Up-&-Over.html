Here are the workout details:

Double Kettlebell Swing x6
Double Kettlebell High Pull x6
Double Kettlebell Snatch x6
Double Kettlebell Push Press x6

Men: Use 16kg-24kg
Women: Use 8kg-16kg
2 to 4 rounds
Rest: Beginner - As necessary; Advanced - W:R=1:2-3

* Work: Rest = 1:2 means time yourself performing this kettlebell complex. Then rest two times as long. Example: It takes you 1 minute to carry out. Rest 2 minutes before doing another set.
