Double Kettlebell Clean And Press Exercises

The Double Kettlebell Clean and Press is a great overall body strength and conditioning exercise. It constructs powerful hips and legs, and strong shoulders, arms, and grip. When made with suitable volume, it has the capability to load on pieces of muscle while all at once conditioning the heart and lungs.

Here's a fast rundown of how you do it safely:

1. Start with the kettlebells in front of you.

2. Strongly trek the kettlebells behind you beneath your hips.

3. Tuck your elbows into your sides and roll the kettlebells around your wrists up the front of your body.

4. Keep the kettlebells pulled near the body.

5. Gently put them in the rack position. Keep the elbows glued to your ribs.

6. Keeping your abs and glutes tight, press the kettlebells up over your head. Push from your lats.

7. Keeping your abs and glutes tight, pull the kettlebells pull back into the rack.

8. Breathe out through the sticking point as you push. Either breathe in as you lower the kettlebells or hold your breath and inhale before your next rep.

9. Re-clean the kettlebells and repeat for the preferred number of reps.

10. Lower the kettlebells to the ground the same way would for the Double Swing.
