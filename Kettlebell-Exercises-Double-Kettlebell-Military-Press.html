Kettlebell Exercises - Double Kettlebell Military Press

The Double Kettlebell Military Press is the undeniable kettlebell upper body strength and muscle building exercise. It is likewise a workout in body mastery-- needing overall body tension. Knowing how to do it properly pays dividends in shoulder, arm, and overall upper body development.

1. Clean the kettlebells into the rack. You may require to bring your feet closer to each other after the Clean.

2. Keeping your abs and glutes tight, press the kettlebells up over your head. Press from your lats.

3. Keeping your abs and glutes tight, pull the kettlebells pull back into the rack.

4. Exhale through the sticking point as you push. Either breathe in as you lower the kettlebells or hold your breath and inhale prior to your next rep.

5. Repeat for the desired variety of reps.

6. Location the kettlebells back on the ground by tossing them back through the legs like a Clean however let the swing out in front of you. Keep your hips back.
