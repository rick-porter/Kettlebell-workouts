Double Kettlebell Complex Workout - Sir Snatch A Lot Double Kettlebell Complex Workout.

Double kettlebell complex workout: For weight loss they are difficult to beat. They're time efficient, and they produce a hormone environment that releases development hormone - one of your main fat loss hormonal agents. Conveniently, growth hormone is also associated with muscle-building, so you get a double value with kettlebell complexes. Plus, you can jack up your conditioning levels too.

A well developed double kettlebell complex workout consists of most of the major movement patterns: hinge, squat, push, pull and can take as little as 10 minutes, depending on how many double kettlebell exercises you construct into them.

This double kettlebell complex workout is among 3 days - the light day - inside a program called, "Sir Snatch A Lot," a double kettlebell snatch based exercise program. It's created mainly for weight loss however can be used to construct muscle too.

*Work: Rest = 1:2 means time yourself performing this kettlebell complex. Then rest twice as long. Example: It takes you 1 minute to perform. Rest 2 minutes before doing another set.
