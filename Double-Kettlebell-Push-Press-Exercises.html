How To Double Kettlebell Push Press

The Double Kettlebell Push Press is a great workout in its own right. It is an effective (pun intended) workout that can overload the shoulder and arm musculature, strengthening the triceps for the lockout of the Military Press.

Moreover, when the volume is high enough, it can be utilized to pack mass on the upper body.

Lastly, when combined with the Double Kettlebell Clean, it's a fantastic alternative to the Double Kettlebell Clean and Jerk for somebody doing not have shoulder movement or the needed ability to drop under the bells for the Jerk.
